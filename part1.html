Are Parrots the Best Kind of Bird?

There are many kinds of birds. They are:

  • Pelicans
  • The Raven
  • Mockingbird
  • Falcon/Eagle
  • Chicken/Peacock

Parrots are a social animal. They have brightly colored feathers and short beaks specced for crushing type damage.
Parrots are the only known bird capable of human-like speech.

Although parrots are not the only bird capable of utilizing tools to achieve objectives — corvids also possess this ability — Parrots are expecially adept at tool use due to their highly dexterous feet. In summary, yes. Parrots are the best kind of bird.
